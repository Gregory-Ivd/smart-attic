Privacy Policy

Ula Calculator  ·  Last updated: June 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

Smart Attic is an independent software studio. This privacy policy covers the Ula Calculator application ("the App") available on Android and Windows.

2. What the App Does

Ula Calculator is a private two-person communication tool designed for end-to-end private messaging and voice calls between exactly two users who share a private room code.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the App:

  • Messages and media — text messages, images, audio, video and documents you send within the App. These are stored on a private server and are accessible only to the two participants of your room.
  • Voice call data — audio is transmitted directly between devices via WebRTC (peer-to-peer) and is not recorded or stored.
  • Room configuration — your chosen username, room code, and room password (stored locally on your device).

4. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Real name, email address, or phone number
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Any data shared with third parties for advertising

5. How Data Is Stored

Messages and uploaded files are stored on a private server hosted on Railway. No data is shared with third parties. Automated weekly backups are created for data integrity purposes and are accessible only to the App operator.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your room exists. You may request deletion of all data associated with your room by contacting us at the address below.

7. Security

All communication between the App and the server is encrypted via HTTPS/WSS (TLS). Room access is protected by a password you choose. The App does not transmit credentials to any third-party service.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
